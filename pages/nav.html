nav

Headers and footers are the same shape — wordmark on the left, links on the right, one hairline border. The wordmark on every kcolbchain page is the same five-character mark; never replace it with a custom logo per product.

breadcrumb

A breadcrumb belongs at the top of a deep view; if the page is one level below a list, you can probably drop it. Use a forward slash as the separator — it reads as a path and pairs with the monospace.

kcolbchain/ depeg-monitor/ history/ USDC 
<div class="crumbs">

</div>
